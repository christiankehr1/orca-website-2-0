The case for ORCA

Four reasons it holds up

01

The document-to-truth moat

Most tools start with data entry. ORCA starts with your documents — extracting the facts and tracing every one back to its source. That's hard to copy, and it's why the truth stays true.

02

You own your information

Your data is yours. Export it, move it, revoke access — any time. ORCA is the custodian of the structure, not the gatekeeper of your data.

03

Privacy as a feature

Encryption and decryption happen in your browser — the password never leaves it. Swiss data centres, zero-knowledge, 256-bit, GDPR. An immutable, blockchain-inspired log.

04

One product, better for everyone

Not a chart tool bolted to a database. One model feeds every view — list, chart, folder, task — so a fix in one place is a fix everywhere.
